Thoth AI

Privacy Policy

Effective: February 13, 2026

Overview

Thoth AI ("the App") is built with privacy at its core. The App runs entirely on your device using on-device language models. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

Data Collection

Thoth AI does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or sign-up is required
  • No personal identifiers are collected (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or tracking data is gathered
  • No advertising identifiers are used
  • No cookies or similar tracking technologies are employed

Your Documents & Data

All documents (PDFs) you import into Thoth AI are processed and stored entirely on your device. This includes:

  • Knowledgebases — created and stored locally in the app's sandboxed storage
  • Embeddings & vector data — generated on-device and stored in a local database
  • Chat history — kept only in the app's local memory during your session
  • LLM inference — all AI processing happens on-device; no queries are sent to any server

Your documents never leave your device. We have no ability to access, read, or retrieve any content you import into the App.

Model Downloads

The App may download AI model files from third-party repositories (such as Hugging Face) when you choose to install a model. These downloads are standard HTTPS requests to fetch publicly available model weights. No personal data is transmitted during this process.

Third-Party Services

Thoth AI does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App does not share any data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

Thoth AI does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since the App collects no personal data from any user, it is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Since we collect no user data, we have no means to notify you directly of changes.

Contact
