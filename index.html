About Axly's Customs

Axly's Customs is a one-person workshop focused on building practical tools that put AI to work in everyday workflows. We're not chasing hype — we build things that solve real problems, from discovering the best new AI tools before they go viral, to running a document assistant that never sends your data to the cloud.

Our flagship product, Goldmine, tracks emerging AI tools and developer repos across six major platforms — GitHub, Hacker News, Product Hunt, Reddit, arXiv, and Dev.to — scoring them by real momentum data instead of marketing buzz. Its companion newsletter, The Shovel, delivers the week's best finds straight to your inbox.

ThothAI takes a different approach: a fully local AI document assistant for Windows and iOS. Upload PDFs, build knowledge bases, and ask questions — all processed on your device with zero cloud dependencies. Your data stays yours.

Before there were AI tools, there was art. Axly's Customs started as a digital art studio, and that creative spirit still runs through everything we make. Whether it's a new product or an original illustration, we're always building something for the curious.

Everything here is built by one person — no VC funding, no growth teams, no enterprise sales. Just tools that work, made for developers, researchers, creators, and anyone who'd rather discover things themselves than wait for an algorithm to decide what's trending. If that sounds like you, poke around. There's always something new in the workshop.
